The Regulatory Assessment Tool is designed to measure compliance with NIS2 Cybersecurity risk-management measures (Article 21).

What is this research about?

Compliance is assessed through a set of multiple-choice questions that evaluate the effectiveness of the NIS2 in-scope controls. Each risk-management measure was mapped to a number of ISO 270001 controls from the 2022 version and guidance from ENISA defines a number of controls which will apply to operators of essential services. These controls are seen as the minimum required security measures.

Why am I doing this research?

This research will measure the effectiveness and usability of the NIS2 Regulatory Assessment Tool by assessing the mapping of the ISO Controls to each of the NIS2 articles and requirements, along with the value of the recommendations provided to ensure compliance with the new directive.

Personal data will not be recorded, and the study is fully anonymous.

What are the benefits of taking part in this research study?

The assessment is a comprehensive gap analysis tool that measures compliance through a set of multiple-choice questions, evaluating the effectiveness of the NIS2 in-scope controls. The tool provides a report of recommendations that can be implemented to improve security posture and overall compliance with the NIS2 Directive.

What are the risks of taking part in this research study?

The assessment may identify areas of weaknesses or vulnerabilities within the users' IT environment.

The NIS2 Cybersecurity Regulatory Assessment Tool is developed for research and informational purposes only.
